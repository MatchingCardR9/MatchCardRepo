How to Play?

1.) First Player Select a starting card. 2.) The second player try to match the card. >> Correct match gives the player + 1 point and it's the player's turn to pick a starting card. / Wrong match takes 1 point from the player, and it's the opponent turn. 3.) The game goes on until there is no card left.
